Hello everybody, we are in group 1 going to present project 2 for BDD200 with topic of
hospital management system.


Luka (Department, Doctors)
- It's a list of departments with the pk dept_id, 
each dept gets its own bugdet and unique phone numbers.

- And dept_id is also being used as the fk for the doctors table,
which is a list of all the doctors registered within the hospital
management system with the pk being the doctor_id.

Khang (Patients, Appointments)
- patients table is a list of all the patients registered under our hospital
management system, with the unique identifier/pk being the patient_id,
and it stores all the personal details about our patients such as fname, 
lname etc.

- patient_id is also being used as the fk for multiple other tables, one
of them being the appointments table.

- this table hold the data about the appointment booking done by the patients,
with their designated doctors, identified by the fk doctor_id.

Rishi (Medical Records, Prescription, Medication)
- Then we have the medical records table, with the pk being the record_id,
alog with two other fk's doctor_id & patient_id. and this table holds the data about the care
administered.

- Then we move on to the prescriptions table which has the pk prec_id, 
its a transactional table that holds doctors accountable for the medication prescribed.

- And at last we have the medications table that hold the data about the stock that the hospital
has.
